Keynote

島田 浩二 (Koji Shimada)
Enishi Tech Inc., Nihon Ruby-no-kai(Japan Ruby Group), RubySapporo.

Koji Shimada is the Co-Founder & CEO of EnishiTech Inc., a board member of Nihon Ruby-no-kai(Japan Ruby Group) and Founder of RubySapporo. He wrote the book "Ruby逆引きレシピ" (with other authors, published by SHOEISHA) and translated Neal Ford's "ProductiveProgrammer" into Japanese (supervisor, The Japanese version, "プロダクティブ・プログラマ" was published by O'Reilly Japan).

About
